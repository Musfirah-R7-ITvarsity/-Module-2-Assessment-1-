PANDA

The panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca), sometimes called a panda bear is a bear species endemic to China. It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body.

The giant panda's diet is primarily herbivorous, consisting almost exclusively of bamboo.
